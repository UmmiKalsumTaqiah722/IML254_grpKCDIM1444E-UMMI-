EXIT : E 1st MINI ALBUM

Fast forward to 2016, WINNER returned after a hiatus of nearly a year and a half since their DEBUT ALBUM [2014 S/S]. On February 1st, as part of their 2016 annual project [EXIT MOVEMENT], they announced the release of their mini album [E].

1st FATE NUMBER FOR 1st SINGLE ALBUM

OUR TWENTY FOR 2nd SINGLE ALBUM

WINNER returned with their new album [OUR TWENTY FOR] four months after receiving continuous love since the release of [FATE NUMBER FOR].[OUR TWENTY FOR] focuses on the youth and explosive love of WINNER in their 20s, staying true to the present.

YEAR RELEASE:2017

EVERYD4Y 2nd FULL ALBUM

Members have broadened their musical spectrum with attempts in various genres such as “hip-hop, trap, acoustic, and ballad.” Combining these musical challenges and concerns with the number <4> symbolizing “WINNER” and meaning “every day,” the album title was put together into a new word [EVERYD4Y].

YEAR RELEASE:2018

MILLIONS 3rd SINGLE ALBUM

The single album “MILLIONS” is a heartwarming Christmas gift for lovers, whispering sweet nothings through its emotional melody, warm lyrics, and rhythmic whistle in the chorus, all blending with a bouncy kick to create a quintessential WINNER winter love song.


YEAR RELEASE:2018

CROSS 3rd MINI ALBUM

[CROSS] encapsulates the relationships, music, and unique story that only WINNER can create as the four members, each possessing their own direction and characteristics, cross paths (X) and unite to form a fresh fusion (+) under the name “WINNER.”



YEAR RELEASE:2019

Remember 3rd FULL ALBUM

Their 3rd full album Remember is a heartfelt creation that aims to cherish the memories shared with their fans. This album features a total of 12 tracks.The album Remember is a precious gift for fans, allowing them to reminisce about the cherished memories spent with WINNER and look forward to the stories that will unfold together in the future.

YEAR RELEASE:2019

HOLIDAY 4th MINI ALBUM

The title track “I LOVE U” is a song that expresses honest emotions of love, capturing WINNER’s unique bright and energetic emotions.In addition to the title track, the album showcases a wide-ranging musical spectrum from WINNER, with rhythmic rapping in hip-hop, soulful vocals in R&B rock, and harmonious soundscapes in dance tracks.
