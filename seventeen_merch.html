11th Mini Album
'Seventeenth Heaven' (AM 5:26 Ver.)

Pledis Entertainment released Seventeenth Heaven, the eleventh extended play (EP) by South Korean boy group Seventeen on October 23, 2023. The eight-track EP, which was produced by Woozi and Bumzu, is a celebration of the band's past accomplishments.

RELEASE YEAR: 2023

VOL.4 'Face The Sun' (Control Ver.)

The fourth studio album of South Korean boy group Seventeen is titled Face the Sun. It was made available on YG Plus on May 27, 2022, by Pledis Entertainment. Nine tracks include the album, with "Hot" acting as the first single. April 15, 2022, saw the pre-release of the first song, "Darl+ing".

RELEASE YEAR: 2022

9th Mini Album - ATTACCA

Pledis Entertainment published Attacca, the eighth extended play (EP) by South Korean boy band Seventeen, on October 22, 2021. Seven tracks are indicated in the EP, with "Rock with You" serving as the lead single.

RELEASE YEAR: 2021

7th Mini Album - HENG:GARAE

The seventh extended play (EP) of South Korean boy band Seventeen is called Heng:garae (헹가래). June 22, 2020, was the release date, according to Pledis Entertainment. There are six tracks total, two of which are singles: "My My" and "Left & Right."

RELEASE YEAR: 2020

4th Album Repackage - SECTOR 17

Sector 17, the album's repackaged version, and its first single, "World," were released on July 18. On July 7, 2022, the fourth song, "Cheers," was made available in advance.

RELEASE YEAR: 2022

17 is Right Here (HERE Ver.)

17 Is Right Here. This is the second Korean-language compilation album that Seventeen released on April 29, 2024. Along with four previously unreleased tracks, including the lead single "Maestro" and the previously unheard instrumental for their debut single "Adore U".

RELEASE YEAR: 2024
