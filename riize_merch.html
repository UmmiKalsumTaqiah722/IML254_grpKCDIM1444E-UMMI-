The 1st Single Album 'Get A Guitar'

The debut single album of South Korean boy band Riize is called "Get a Guitar". The single album "Memories" and its title track were both published on August 21 and September 4, respectively, to promote the single album. It was released on September 4, 2023, by SM Entertainment.

RELEASE YEAR: 2023

The 1st Mini Album 'RIIZING'

They recently released The First Mini Album on June 17. Compiling all of the rookie group's releases together with an extra track called "Boom Boom Bass," the album shows their versatility and openness to experimenting with other genres.  

RELEASE YEAR: 2024
