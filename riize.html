RIIZE

Background

Riize is a boy group under SM Entertainment that has seven members. They made their debut on September 4, 2023, with the single album ‘Get A Guitar‘. The members consist of Shotaro, Eunseok, Sungchan, Wonbin, Seunghan, Sohee, and Anton.  

RIIZE LOGO

RIIZE & REALIZE

The group name Riize is created from the combination of "Rise,"  which means "growth,"  and "Realise," which indicates "a team that grows together and realises dreams."
