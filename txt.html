TXT

Background

TXT, an acronym for Tomorrow X Together, is a five-member South Korean boy band that debuted under Big Hit Entertainment in March 2019. The group consists of members Yeonjun, Soobin, Beomgyu, Taehyun, and Huening Kai. TXT quickly established themselves as a prominent new force in the K-pop industry with their debut EP, "The Dream Chapter: Star," which received widespread acclaim and commercial success. Their music, characterized by a vibrant mix of genres, relatable themes, and visually striking performances, has resonated with a diverse audience, contributing to their rapid rise in popularity. Known as the younger brother group to BTS, TXT has carved out their own identity, earning a loyal fanbase called MOA (Moments of Alwaysness).

TXT LOGO

TXT is an acronym for Tomorrow X Together. Its concept stands for “five people united by one dream in the hope of building a better tomorrow.” Nevertheless, short characters fit perfectly into the laws of the modern scene and look incredibly impressive – “+ X +.” The two-plus signs around the edges represent the letters “T” and “X” mathematical symbols for multiplication. Another interpretation of the name: “We are all different, but harmoniously form one synergy.”
