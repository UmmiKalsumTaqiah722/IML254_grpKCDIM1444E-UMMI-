TWS

Background

TWS is a six members boy group formed under Pledis Entertainment after 9 years Seventeen debut. They made their debut on January 22, 2024 with their mini album "Sparkling Blue".  

TWS LOGO

The group's name stands for "Twenty-Four Seven with Us" and their afficial motto is: "Twenty-Four Seven with Us! Hello, we are TWS."
