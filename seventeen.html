SEVENTEEN

Background

Seventeen often shortened as SVT, is a thirteen members boy group under Pledis Entertainment. They made their official debut on May 26, 2015 with the mini album 17 Carat.

Seventeen members divided into three units:~

1. Vocal unit
This unit consists five members Jeonghan, Joshua, Woozi, DoKyeom and Seungkwan.

2. HipHop unit
Meanwhile, this unit consists of four members Scoups, WonWoo, Mingyu and Vernon.

3. Performance unit
Lastly, this unit consists of four members too like Hoshi, Jun, The8 and Dino.

FunFact: Seventeen's name is said to be derived from "13 members + 3 units + 1 group" expressing how 13 individuals from three units come together to form one powerful group.

FANDOM

On February 14, 2016, Seventeen held an exclusive concert 'LIKE SEVENTEEN - Boys Wish' where they revealed their official fan club name, following their performances for "Rock," "NO F.U.N" and "Mansae." Once the performance was over, Leader S.Coups said, "We have an announcement. You guys made us shine so bright. So you guys are now going to be our CARAT."
