BIGBANG

Background

Bigbang is a very popular Korean boy band. Earlier, they had five members: T.O.P., G-dragon, Taeyang, Daesung, and Seungri, but now Seungri has left the group because of his issue with the burning sun. Bigbang was launched by the famous management company YG Entertainment on August 19, 2006, and people also called them the King of K-pop because of their distinct music style, consistent album sales, sold-out concerts, their fashion, and even though their name is still well known around the world.
