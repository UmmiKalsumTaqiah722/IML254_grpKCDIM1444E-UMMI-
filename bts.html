BTS

Background

BTS, also known as Bangtan Sonyeondan or Beyond The Scene, is a seven-member South Korean boy band that debuted under Big Hit Entertainment (now HYBE Corporation) in June 2013. The group, comprising RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook, quickly rose to international fame with their unique blend of music that combines hip-hop, pop, and R&B, alongside their powerful performances and socially conscious lyrics. BTS is renowned not only for their musical talent but also for their deep connection with their global fanbase, known as ARMY. Their influence extends beyond music into various aspects of global culture, earning them numerous accolades and recognition worldwide.

BTS LOGO

The BTS logo features two trapezoids that symbolize doors, with the meaning being “ARMY meeting BTS at the doors.
